👥 Body Doubling — How It Works
Share your room code with a friend, colleague, or anyone you want to focus with. When they join using your code, you'll both appear in the room and can see each other's status. Work silently together — the presence alone is enough.

No camera. No microphone required. Just silent accountability.

Evidence: Solanto (2011) — body doubling is one of the most universally endorsed ADHD strategies. The presence of another person activates regulatory circuits that internal willpower cannot.
