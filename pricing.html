Frequently Asked Questions
Is this a clinical ADHD diagnosis? ▾
No. ADHDclearfocus is an educational screening and wellness tool — it does not constitute a clinical diagnosis of ADHD or any other condition. Our 39-question screener incorporates validated clinical frameworks (ASRS-v1.1, Brown, DIVA-5, Barkley) and is the most comprehensive self-assessment available online, but it is not a substitute for formal clinical assessment by a qualified psychologist or psychiatrist. If your profile suggests clinically significant ADHD, our report includes a clear guide to formal assessment pathways in Ireland and the UK.
Can I use this to support a GP or psychiatry referral? ▾
Yes — many users bring their ADHDclearfocus report to their GP appointment to support a referral request. The report's 10-dimension profile, clinical framework references, and formal assessment pathway section are specifically designed to be useful in that conversation. It cannot replace the formal assessment but can strengthen the case for referral and give your GP useful background.
What's the difference between the screener and the Clarity Report? ▾
The screener is free — you complete all 39 questions and see your overall profile level and dimension scores. The Clarity Report (€49) unlocks your full 14-page PDF with: AI-generated personalised narrative for your specific profile, detailed per-dimension analysis with evidence-based strategies, formal assessment pathway guide for Ireland and the UK, and your lifetime loyalty code for Pro subscription.
How long does the report take to arrive? ▾
Typically within 2 hours of payment. The AI analysis and PDF generation are automated — triggered immediately when your Stripe payment is confirmed. If you haven't received your report within 24 hours, check your spam folder and email us at conalldonegan@outlook.com.
What is the 15% clinical assessment discount? ▾
Pro subscribers (yearly plan) receive an exclusive 15% discount on formal ADHD and ASD clinical assessments through our registered partner network. Partners include psychologists and psychiatrists registered with HSE, NHS, CORU, and BPS. Your unique discount code is sent with your Pro welcome email. The assessment must meet DSM-5/ICD-11 diagnostic criteria to qualify.
Is my data private and GDPR compliant? ▾
Yes. Your screener responses are processed in your browser and are not stored on our servers unless you proceed to purchase. We collect your email address to deliver your report and, with your explicit consent, send educational content. Your health data is processed under Article 9 of the EU GDPR with your explicit consent. You can request deletion of your data at any time. See our full Privacy Policy.
Does ADHDclearfocus work for ASD / autism? ▾
The current screener focuses on ADHD across 10 neurological dimensions. Several dimensions — including rejection sensitivity, emotional regulation, and hyperfocus — overlap significantly with autism presentations. A dedicated ASD screening tool is in development. ADHDclearfocus Pro subscribers will receive early access when launched, and the existing 15% clinical discount applies to ASD assessments as well as ADHD.
Can I get a refund? ▾
You have a 7-day right of withdrawal under the Consumer Rights Act 2022 (Ireland). This right is waived once the PDF report has been downloaded or accessed. If you have not accessed your report and wish to cancel, email conalldonegan@outlook.com within 7 days. Pro subscription cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing period — no partial refunds.
I'm a parent of a teenager with ADHD. Is this suitable? ▾
Yes — the screener includes teen-adapted question variants and is appropriate for ages 13+. The strategies, crisis tools, and community are designed for adults and older teenagers. If completing for a teenager under 18, parental/guardian awareness is recommended. For children under 13, please consult your GP or paediatrician directly.
