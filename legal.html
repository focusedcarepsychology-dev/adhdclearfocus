ADHDclearfocus is operated by Focused Care Psychology Limited (FCP Ltd), a company registered in Ireland. Our registered address is Waterford, Ireland. For all privacy-related queries, contact us at conalldonegan@outlook.com.

2. What data we collect

Screener responses: Your answers to the 22 screener questions are processed to generate your profile results and PDF report. These are processed in your browser session and are not stored on our servers unless you proceed to purchase.

Email address: Collected when you request your results or purchase a report. Used to deliver your report and, with your consent, to send educational content about ADHD.

Payment data: Payments are processed by Stripe. We do not store your card details. Stripe's privacy policy applies to payment processing.

Usage data: We collect anonymised usage analytics (page views, completion rates) via Google Analytics. No individual user is identified in this data.

3. Special category data

Information about your ADHD profile constitutes special category health data under Article 9 of the EU GDPR. We process this data on the basis of your explicit consent, which you provide when you submit your screener responses and request your results.

You may withdraw consent at any time by contacting us at conalldonegan@outlook.com. Withdrawal of consent does not affect the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal.

4. Legal basis for processing

Consent (Article 6(1)(a) and Article 9(2)(a)): Processing your screener responses and health-related profile data.

Contract (Article 6(1)(b)): Processing your email address and payment information to fulfil your purchase of the Clarity Report or Pro subscription.

Legitimate interests (Article 6(1)(f)): Anonymised analytics to improve our service, where this does not override your rights.

5. How we use your data

To deliver your screener results and PDF report to your email address.

To manage your subscription and billing if you have a Pro membership.

To send educational content about ADHD, where you have consented to receive this.

To improve our screener and report content using anonymised, aggregated data.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

6. Data retention

Email addresses and purchase records are retained for 7 years for legal and accounting purposes, then deleted.

Screener responses linked to your email are retained for the duration of your subscription plus 12 months, then deleted.

Anonymised analytics data is retained indefinitely as it cannot identify individuals.

You may request deletion of your data at any time by emailing conalldonegan@outlook.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Your rights under GDPR

You have the right to: access your personal data; correct inaccurate data; request deletion of your data; object to processing; request restriction of processing; data portability; and withdraw consent at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, email conalldonegan@outlook.com with the subject line "Data Rights Request". We will respond within 30 days.

You have the right to lodge a complaint with the Irish Data Protection Commission (DPC) at dataprotection.ie or +353 57 868 4800.

8. Cookies

We use essential cookies only — session management and security. Google Analytics uses anonymised cookies for usage data. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party marketing cookies.

9. International transfers

Your data is processed within the European Economic Area (EEA). Where we use service providers outside the EEA (such as Stripe and Google Analytics), we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place under Article 46 GDPR (Standard Contractual Clauses).
