Evidence: An RCT of adult ADHD patients found significant improvement in subjective study performance using 15Hz beats vs placebo (Malandrone et al., 2022, European Psychiatry). A 2024 systematic review found binaural beats reduce anxiety better than silence or noise-cancelling headphones alone (Baseanu et al., 2024, Applied Sciences). ⚡ Grounding Tools
5️⃣ 5-4-3-2-1 Senses
5

Name 5 things you can see right now. Look slowly around the room.

4

Name 4 things you can touch. Feel the texture of each one.

3

Name 3 things you can hear. Listen for sounds in the background.

2

Name 2 things you can smell. Or breathe slowly and notice the air.

1

Name 1 thing you can taste. Or notice the sensation in your mouth.

⬜ Box Breathing — 4-4-4-4
1

Breathe in through your nose for 4 counts. Fill your lungs completely.

2

Hold at the top for 4 counts. Stay still.

3

Breathe out slowly through your mouth for 4 counts. Empty fully.

4

Hold at the bottom for 4 counts. Then repeat from step 1.

Repeat 4-6 times. US Navy SEALs use this technique for acute stress control.
🌊 RAIN Technique
R

Recognise — What am I feeling right now? Name it precisely. Not "stressed" — "I feel overwhelmed and afraid."

A

Allow — Let the feeling be there without fighting it. "This is here. That's okay."

I

Investigate — Where do you feel this in your body? What triggered it? What does it need?

N

Non-identify — "I am not this feeling. I am the awareness noticing it."

🦶 Feel Your Feet
1

Press your feet flat on the floor. Feel the pressure.

2

Notice the weight of your body in your chair or against the surface beneath you.

3

Press your palms together firmly for 5 seconds. Release slowly.

4

Take 3 slow breaths. With each exhale, consciously relax your jaw, shoulders, and hands.

Physical grounding activates the parasympathetic nervous system. Proprioceptive input (pressure, resistance) counteracts the fight-or-flight response.
💔 RSD — Rejection Sensitivity Response
1

Recognise it: "This intense pain is my ADHD nervous system generating a disproportionate response. It is not an accurate measure of what happened."

2

24-hour rule: Do not respond to what triggered this for 24 hours. The RSD response is immediate but almost always inaccurate. Time allows prefrontal reappraisal.

3

Counter-statement: "My worth is not determined by this person's opinion or this moment's outcome."

4

Physical reset: Cold water on your wrists or face. Physical sensation breaks the emotional loop.

RSD affects ~99% of adults with ADHD. The pain is neurologically real — and neurologically generated, not situationally proportionate (Dodson, 2019).
